Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose colours?

Choosing colours is part of our job.With the help of the design brief (where you as a client have significant input) as well as our design research, we will present you with colour scheme options for your design. And don’t worry, if you have a specific colour you want us to use or must use, we’ll ensure that our designs work well with that colour.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept payment in the form of cash or electronic transfer.

Will I be able to update these files on my computer?

Unless you use a computer with the relevant software, you will not be able to make alterations.

Who owns the copyright for the designs you produce?

Copyright for all work carried out by Creative Pels transfers to the client as soon as the balance has been settled in full. We do, however, reserve the right to use any work for promotional purposes.

I need something designed at a very short time, is this possible?

Sometimes customers require our services at very short notice. We can usually accommodate rush jobs depending on the size of the project and how full our production schedule is. These jobs are usually subjected to an additional charge on the flat hourly rate.

I'm starting a new business and I don’t have a big budget. Can you help me?

Yes, we can help your start-up business. We can create a branding package to help you kick-start your business; including , Banners and social media graphics, Flyers, Business cards and so on.
